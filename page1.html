Top 5 Best Movies of All Time

Avatar (2009)

Directed by James Cameron, this epic sci-fi adventure is set on the alien planet Pandora, where a paraplegic Marine teams up with its inhabitants in a battle for valuable resources.

Box Office Gross: $2.924 billion worldwide

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this iconic crime drama follows the powerful Italian-American Mafia family, the Corleones.

Casablanca (1942)

A classic romantic drama set during World War II, starring Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman.

Seven Samurai (1954)

Akira Kurosawa's masterpiece about a group of samurai defending a village against bandits.

Parasite (2019)

Bong Joon-ho's dark comedy-thriller explores class dynamics when a poor family infiltrates the lives of a wealthy household.
